## TL;DR — Moldova IT market in numbers

- **Moldova IT Park (MITP) turnover 2024:** €770M (+21% YoY); projected €1B in 2025.
- **MITP residents:** 2,725 companies (+33% YoY 2024); **24,200+ specialists** in MITP, +~9K outside.
- **Export share of revenue:** 88%. **% of Moldova's services exports:** 26%. **% of GDP:** 4.7%.
- **Average gross salary:** 50,064 MDL/мес (~€2,600), +23% YoY.
- **Tax regime:** 7% единый от оборота, locked until 2035 — the structural moat for any Moldovan corporate vehicle.
- **Sector growth:** highest % growth in CEE in 2023; absolute size ~20× smaller than Romania, ~½ Ukrainian outsourcing alone.
- **Direct competitive set under hybrid C-model:** essentially empty — 2–3 partial matches.

**Top export destinations:** US (#1), UK, France, Germany, Netherlands, Romania (fast-growing post-2023 due to Romanian IT exemption revocation).

**Growth dynamic:** Ukraine war (Feb 2022) was net positive for Moldovan IT — Ukrainian companies relocated for safety, Romanian companies relocated after Bucharest cancelled IT tax exemptions in 2023, Moldova hosted highest per-capita Ukrainian refugee inflow (~10%) which broadened dev labor pool. Sector grew double-digit every year 2021–2024, even as overall Moldovan GDP contracted.

**Gap in data:** ATIC has not published a sector breakdown (BPO vs custom dev vs products vs consulting) since the 2021 Policy White Book. Total all-Moldova IT revenue (including non-MITP firms) is not officially published; the likely 2024 figure is €900M–€1B but no primary source states it directly.

## 2. Who dominates the market

Three structural tiers:

**Tier 1 (1,000+ headcount in Moldova):** All foreign-owned or captive — they compete in London/SF/Paris, not on Moldovan soil.

- **Endava Moldova** — ~1,200 (UK PLC, NYSE:DAVA). Outsourced custom dev, EU banking & payments. Bodyshop. ([source](https://kitalent.com/articles/article-chisinau-financial-services-eu-paradox/))
- **Dyninno Group** (Trevolution / Ecofinance) — ~1,400, US-headquartered. Captive product company (ASAP Tickets, Multipass, etc.). ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dyninno_Group))
- **Orange Systems** — ~800–850, French foreign branch. Intra-Orange-Group only, never on open market. ([systems.orange.md](https://systems.orange.md/about/))

**Tier 2 (200–600 headcount):** A pack of foreign-owned delivery centers — bodyshops with foreign capital, Moldovan engineers, no original brand or methodology to defend.

- **Stefanini Moldova** — ~300, Brazilian. ([romania-insider](https://www.romania-insider.com/brazilian-group-stefanini-opens-office-moldovas-capital))
- **Coherent Solutions Moldova** — 300–500, US (Minneapolis). ([coherentsolutions.md](https://coherentsolutions.md/))
- **Software Mind Romania & Moldova** (ex-Code Factory) — ~244, Polish (Ailleron). ([linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com/company/codefactorygroup))
- **Pentalog Chișinău** (now Globant) — ~241, Argentinian/US. ([pentalog](https://www.pentalog.com/locations/chisinau-nearshore-delivery-center/))
- **Cedacri International** — 200–400, Italian (ION Group). Banking software, vertical-locked.
- **Amdaris** (now Insight) — 150–300, US (Insight Enterprises NASDAQ:NSIT). The most "agency-shaped" of the foreign-owned dev shops.
- **Crystal System Group** — Cyprus-headquartered, multi-country.
- **Allied Testing** — 200–400, UK/US. Specialty: capital markets QA.
- **3Pillar Global Moldova** — 100–200, US. Product engineering.
- **StarNet** — ~600 across telecom group. 100% local Moldovan. Telecom + enterprise ICT. Not in our competitive set (telecom infra).
- **Simpals** — 201–500, **100% local** (Dmitri Voloshin). Own products (999.md, Point.md, Lobster, animation, Chișinău Marathon). The only Moldovan org running a product/lab model at scale.

**Tier 3 (<200, 2,700+ companies):** Local-owned dev shops and freelancer-LLCs riding the 7% MITP tax regime. Notable:

- **Indrivo** — ~80 across MD+RO. Local-owned founders. Custom software, government/EU work, built Tekwill platform. **Closest local-owned services firm with own positioning.** ([indrivo.com](https://indrivo.com/about/our-story))
- **EBS Integrator** — 50–100 local. Borderline bodyshop/agency. AmCham member.
- **DAS Solutions** — acquired by Cegeka (Belgium) 2021; now ~25–40 as Cegeka delivery.
- **NetInfo, EST Computer** — small custom dev for finance/banking/healthcare.

## 3. Direct competitive set under C-model

In contrast to top-15 (which compete elsewhere), the actual competitive set on the "Moldovan studio with own brand and methodology" axis is **vanishingly small**:

## 4. Where the gap is

Mapping the 15+ studios above against the RS hybrid C-model (engagement services + Muse spec authoring + Forge autonomous pipeline + opinionated design):

| Dimension | Filled in MD? | By whom |
|---|---|---|
| Boutique brand-only for tech/SaaS founders, EN-first | Yes | Livada Studio |
| Mid-market full-cycle agency, multi-language | Yes | LE BURO, Nikba, Kooperativa, KUKU |
| Wine/FMCG packaging | Heavily filled | Bonsanco, 43'oz, Cutline |
| Enterprise dev shop with AI service line | Yes | Amigo, Oneest, FusionWorks |
| Pure ML/AI dev (custom models) | Thin but present | Cortex, Quasar Labs, Scalek |
| **Studio that publicly ships its own AI tooling alongside client work** | **No one** | — |
| **Founder-led hybrid: opinionated brand + dev + agentic infra** | **No one** | — |
| **Studio with public methodology / spec-driven process / open-source posture** | **No one** | — |
| **English-first, internationally-targeted, dev+design hybrid** | **Almost no one** (Livada brand-only; Doubles brand-only; Eligence dev-only) | — |

**The gap RS can own in Moldova:**

1. **Hybrid C-model is genuinely empty.** No one publicly running both engagement services and a public-tooling/lab arm. Simpals does it at 200-person scale; nobody does it at studio scale (2–30).
2. **No studio has public methodology IP.** No Moldovan studio publishes specs, frameworks, design principles, or open tooling. RS's `openspec/` discipline alone is differentiation.
3. **Livada is the nearest peer but capped on the design-only ceiling** — they sell to AI/SaaS founders but don't build products. RS can take the next step Livada cannot: ship the product *and* build the brand.
4. **EN-first marketing to international tech founders is wide open** — most MD studios anchor in RO or RU and serve regional clients.
5. **AI-as-deliverable vs. AI-as-internal-leverage** — MD AI shops sell ML services to clients. Nobody is publicly using agentic infra (Forge-style) to multiply studio throughput while also offering productized engagements.

**What's NOT a gap:** wine/FMCG packaging (saturated), local SMB lead-gen marketing (saturated), enterprise outsourcing (Endava et al. own it).

## 5. Strategic implications for RS

1. **On Moldovan soil there are no real competitors.** The competitive question shifts from "who do we beat in MD?" to "we don't compete in MD locally — we use MD as base and address international audiences."
2. **EN-first marketing is critical.** Livada is already doing this (correctly). Doubles, ancora — trying. Most others target local. RS должен быть в EN-first группе по умолчанию; RU/RO как secondary для diaspora-network.
3. **Methodology-as-marketing — пустая ниша целиком.** OpenSpec discipline + публичные case-studies использования Muse/Forge — мгновенный differentiator.
4. **Местный engagement-pricing — не reference.** Молдавские студии прячут цены или продают по local-SMB ставкам. Наш потолок — international founder-engagement rates, не молдавские.
5. **Simpals — operational benchmark.** Не на главной, не цитируем. Но как референс "что делает молдавская команда, когда строит lab + products successfully" — единственный пример рядом.
6. **MD SRL остаётся** как корпоративный vehicle — 7% MITP до 2035 структурно лучше любого EU-режима (см. [`competitor-landscape-europe.md`](./competitor-landscape-europe.md) § Tax landscape).
